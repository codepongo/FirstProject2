00024         b. Define <PACKETNAME>PACKETDATA and <PACKETNAME>PACKETMODE similar to

00030    for each extension:
00031         a. Before including pktdef.h, define <PACKETNAME>PACKET<EXTENSION>
00032            as either PKEXT_ABSOLUTE or PKEXT_RELATIVE.
00033         b. The generated structure typedef will contain a field for the
00034            extension data.
00035         c. Scan the WTI_EXTENSION categories to find the extension's
00036            packet mask bit.
00037         d. OR the packet mask bit with <PACKETNAME>PACKETDATA and use the
00038            result in the lcPktData field of the LOGCONTEXT structure.
00039         e. If <PACKETNAME>PACKET<EXTENSION> was PKEXT_RELATIVE, OR the
00040            packet mask bit with <PACKETNAME>PACKETMODE and use the result
